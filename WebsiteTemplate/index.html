Meet SowGrow,

A non-profit organization that provides help towards Third World Entrepreneurs grow into a stable business, helping the world one seed at a time! :)

Our Goal


Our goal is to bring sustainable business education, practices, and resources to entrepreneurs in underdeveloped countries.


We also provided a P2P microfinancing platform for business owners who can take advantage of extra capital. With this extra capital, businesses around the world can easily help other businesses with the click of a button, and where they can make a major impact.

Mentorship Program


The focus of our mentorship program is on business advising and resources in underdeveloped countries. We strive to provide a foundational business education and advise business owners on next steps they can take to grow and provide more value in their country. For a sustainable business model, we require business owners who have been given advising resources to advise other entrepreneurs in their area.
